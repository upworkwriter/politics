Frequently Asked Questions

What are the main obstacles faced in enforcing legislation?
The main obstacles include lack of resources, difficulties in monitoring compliance, and resistance from individuals or organizations.
How can enforcement agencies effectively address these challenges?
Enforcement agencies can address these challenges by increasing funding and manpower, improving technology for monitoring, and collaborating with other agencies.
What are the potential consequences of ineffective enforcement of legislation?
Potential consequences include non-compliance leading to harm or damage, erosion of public trust in the legal system, and diminishing deterrent effect on future violations.
